Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • Punch of salt
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 20g Butter
  • 20g Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add a punch of salt
  3. Cook until al dente
  4. While the spaghetti is cooking fry garlic in butter
  5. Drain the water from the spaghetti and toss in the pan with the garlic
  6. Plate up and enjoy :)
